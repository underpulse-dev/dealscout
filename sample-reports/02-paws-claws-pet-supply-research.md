## Opportunities Identified

1. **E-commerce Integration**
   - Launch online store with local delivery
   - "Shop local, delivered fast" positioning
   - Compete with Chewy on convenience + local service

2. **Subscription/Loyalty Program**
   - Auto-delivery subscription (compete with Chewy)
   - Loyalty rewards (10% back, free bag every 10th)
   - VIP club with exclusive products

3. **Services Expansion**
   - Add grooming (high-margin, recurring)
   - Mobile grooming service
   - Pet training classes (community building)
   - Pet photography days

4. **Premium Positioning**
   - Lean into high-end, specialty products
   - Raw food/fresh food offerings
   - Local Austin pet food brands (differentiation)

5. **Community Events**
   - Pet adoption days (partner with shelters)
   - Dog birthday parties
   - Breed meetups
   - "Yappy Hours" (social events)

6. **Store Refresh**
   - Modernize interior (customers noted dated look)
   - Instagram-worthy photo spots
   - Dog-friendly layout improvements

## Due Diligence Priorities

1. **Financial verification** - 3 years tax returns, P&L, understand e-commerce impact
2. **Lease review** - Rent amount, terms, South Lamar is expensive
3. **Inventory analysis** - Turn rates, spoilage, vendor relationships
4. **Competition assessment** - Visit PetSmart/Petco, understand pricing gap
5. **Customer base** - Concentration, loyalty, repeat rate
6. **Supplier contracts** - Access to premium brands (often exclusive)
7. **Owner transition** - How dependent is business on current owner's relationships?
