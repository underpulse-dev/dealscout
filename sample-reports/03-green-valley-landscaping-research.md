## Due Diligence Priorities

1. **Customer concentration** - How many customers = 80% of revenue? Risk if concentrated
2. **Contract verification** - Review maintenance contracts, terms, renewal rates
3. **Equipment condition** - Inspect trucks, mowers, tools (replacement costs?)
4. **Employee status** - W-2 or 1099? Immigration compliance? Key crew retention?
5. **Financial verification** - 3 years tax returns, understand seasonality patterns
6. **License & insurance** - ROC license transferable? Insurance adequate?
7. **Owner transition** - Can business survive without current owner's oversight?
