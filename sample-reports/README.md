## Report Structure (Full Report - $199)

### Cover Page
- Business name, location, asking price
- DealScout logo, report date
- Confidentiality notice

### Executive Summary (1 page)
- Business overview
- Key findings (3-5 bullets)
- Risk rating (Low/Medium/High)
- Overall recommendation

### Section 1: Business Overview (1 page)
- Industry, location, asking price
- Revenue/profitability (if available)
- Years in operation
- Current ownership structure

### Section 2: Online Reputation Analysis (2 pages)
- Review sentiment analysis (Google, Yelp, Facebook)
- Star ratings trend over time
- Common themes in positive/negative reviews
- Customer complaint patterns
- Response to negative reviews

### Section 3: Competitive Landscape (2 pages)
- Direct competitors (3-5 identified)
- Market saturation analysis
- Competitive positioning
- Price comparison
- Unique value proposition assessment

### Section 4: Market & Demographics (1-2 pages)
- Location demographics (income, age, education)
- Population trends
- Local economic indicators
- Target customer profile fit

### Section 5: Risk Assessment (1-2 pages)
- Reputation risks (negative patterns, controversies)
- Market risks (saturation, declining trends)
- Operational risks (owner-dependency, staff issues)
- Legal/compliance risks (permits, violations)
- Financial red flags

### Section 6: Opportunities & Recommendations (1 page)
- Growth opportunities identified
- Quick wins for new owner
- Areas needing attention
- Due diligence priorities

### Appendix
- Data sources
- Methodology
- Limitations & disclaimers

## Report Generation Process

For each business:
1. Research real market data (Census, demographics)
2. Find comparable real businesses on Google/Yelp
3. Analyze review patterns from similar businesses
4. Use Claude to synthesize findings
5. Generate professional PDF using WeasyPrint (or manual formatting first)
