PetSmart location 0.3 miles away creates direct price competition. Budget-conscious customers may shop PetSmart for products and only use Woof Gang for grooming.

STAFFING RISK (GROOMER DEPENDENCY)

Reddit and reviews specifically mention groomer "Melissa" by name. If Melissa leaves, business could lose customers.
