Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get my report?

Quick Scans are delivered within 10 minutes. Full Reports typically take 15-30 minutes depending on data availability.

What if the business I'm interested in isn't online?

DealScout works best for businesses with an online presence (reviews, website, social media). If your target has minimal online footprint, we may not be able to generate a comprehensive report. We'll refund you 100% if we can't deliver.

Is this better than doing my own research?

DealScout is a pre-screening tool that automates hours of public-data research. It helps you decide which businesses are worth pursuing before you invest in professional due diligence ($15K-$30K). Think of it as the Carfax check before the mechanic's inspection.

What's your refund policy?

100% refund if we can't generate your report. 50% refund within 48 hours if you're unsatisfied with the quality. No refunds after 48 hours or after downloading the report.

Do you work with business brokers?

Currently we serve individual buyers directly. If you're a broker interested in white-label reports for your clients, email hello@dealscout.ai
